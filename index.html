Who all can Register?

IEEE or Non-IEEE members in any under-graduation or graduation program from any institute can register for Explorica 6.0.

You can register online through the link provided on the website or through our official social media handles.

Definitely! All participants will receive valid certificates for attending Explorica 6.0.

Yes! We have exciting pre-events, workshops, and speaker sessions led by experts.

Yes, all activities are scheduled to take place at LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Trivandrum.
